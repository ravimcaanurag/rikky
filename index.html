Overview


The destiny of a nation depends largely upon the individuals who have the capacity to show consistently high performance and productivity in the spheres of their pursuits. Higher Education has a special and crucial role in the building of a nation. The potent human resource of a nation must emerge from HEIs. The current education scenario requires it that stakeholders of education ought of focus on tapping the unexplored potentialities of the learners. The conventional classroom techniques and learning conditions are to be effectively integrated with Information and Communication Technologies (ICTs) to empower teaching-learning competencies. The teacher’s role shifts from a mere provider of knowledge to a facilitator and the student’s role from a passive listener to an ‘autonomous learner.’ ‘Quality Individuals, Quality Institute and Quality India’-are the three key factors of a knowledge society to emerge.
